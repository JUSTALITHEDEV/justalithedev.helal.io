Our Story

Helal was born out of a shared passion for coding and innovation among a group of school friends. What started as after-school coding sessions quickly evolved into a team of young developers taking on real-world projects.

Founded in 2025, we've grown from working on small school projects to developing solutions for businesses and organizations. Our journey reflects our commitment to continuous learning and excellence in technology.

We believe age is just a number when it comes to creating impactful digital solutions. Every line of code we write is a step toward our vision of becoming leading developers in our region.
