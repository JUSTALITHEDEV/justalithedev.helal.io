Our Story

Helal was founded in 2025 by high school developer Ali Ezz, who transformed his passion for technology into a thriving digital solutions provider. What began as after-school coding sessions quickly evolved into a professional service helping businesses solve real-world problems.

Despite our young roots, we approach every project with professional rigor and commitment to excellence. We've proven that age is no barrier to delivering enterprise-grade solutions, having successfully completed projects for clients ranging from local startups to established organizations.

Our unique perspective as young technologists allows us to bring fresh, innovative approaches to digital challenges while maintaining the reliability and quality expected from professional developers.

Founder

Ali Ezz

Founder & Lead Developer

A self-taught programmer and tech entrepreneur, Ali launched Helal while still in high school to pursue his vision of building impactful digital solutions. As lead developer, he oversees all technical operations while continuing to code daily.

Specializing in full-stack development and system architecture, Ali ensures Helal's projects meet professional standards while incorporating cutting-edge technologies. His work has been recognized by local tech communities and educational institutions.
